Greetings fellow Illini,

The UIUC Senate's Committee on Committees is seeking to nominate interested students to serve on the campus Athletic Board.  The Athletic Board serves as an advisory committee to the Chancellor and the Athletic Director on the fiscal operational aspects of the varsity sports program. The Board is also responsible for these programs as they relate to the academic and educational objectives defined by the Senate.

At the March meeting of the Senate, the Committee on Committees will present a slate of four student nominees. Upon Senate approval of the slate, the Chancellor will appoint two of these nominees.

If you are interested in serving on the Athletic Board, we invite you to apply via the link below by Wednesday, March 7th at 11:59pm.

https://illinois.edu/fb/sec/1295270

Any questions may be sent to senate@illinois.edu or maskeri1@illinois.edu
