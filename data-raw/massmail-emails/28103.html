VOTE NOW!

Voting is now open for the Spring 2017 Student Trustee election. The ballot is open until 11:59pm on Thursday, April 27, 2017.

The ballot can be accessed on any computer with internet access; you do not need to be on the University network. During peak times, the ballot may be slow to load. Common technical questions are addressed on the Voting FAQ page. If you have any additional questions or comments, you may e-mail studentelections@illinois.edu.

Thank you for participating in this important part of shared governance at the University of Illinois.
