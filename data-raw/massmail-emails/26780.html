Greetings fellow Illini,

Interested in having a voice in the development of majors, minors, and the academic calendar?  Want to discuss and rule on student discipline cases? Desire a vote on the approval of General Education courses or changes to the Student Code?  Then consider serving on a committee of the Academic Faculty-Student Senate - you do not need to be a student senator to serve!

The Academic Senate's Committee on Committees is seeking to nominate interested students to serve on seventeen different committees, councils, and boards.  If you are interested in serving on any of these entities, we invite you to apply via the link below by Wednesday, March 27th at 11:59pm.

https://illinois.edu/fb/sec/326493

For information on Academic Senate Committees, go to:  http://senate.illinois.edu/bylaws_d.asp
For information on the General Education Board, go to:  http://senate.illinois.edu/bylaws_gb.asp
For information on the Military Education Council, go to:  http://senate.illinois.edu/bylaws_f.asp

Any questions may be sent to senate@illinois.edu or maskeri1@illinois.edu.

Thank you,
