Dear members of the campus community,

I invite you to join me for a town hall meeting next Monday, Oct. 1, at 4 p.m. in the Alice Campbell Alumni Center ballroom. The meeting is part of an ongoing effort to allow campus wide participation in guiding the future of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I will provide an update on our Visioning Future Excellence effort and answer your questions about the process.

I hope that you can attend. This is an approved event for civil service employees. That means employees may attend, without loss of pay or having to charge a benefit, with prior supervisory approval and operations permitting.

If you are not able to be there, the event will be streamed live on the 'Here and Now' videos section of the Illinois homepage or directly from this link on Monday: http://go.illinois.edu/townhalloctober1.

Sincerely,

Phyllis

Phyllis M. Wise
Vice President, University of Illinois
Chancellor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
