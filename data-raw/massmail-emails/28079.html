Dear Colleagues:

The memo in the link shown below has been prepared by Executive Director Lindsay Anderson and the Office of Governmental Relations to make sure that all University employees who engage in lobbying, as defined by the Federal Lobbying Disclosure Act (LDA), properly disclose the necessary information.

Please review the lobbying disclosure memo, visit the website listed in the memo and complete the lobbying disclosure form if you have had contact with a federal official or congressional staff member. The reporting deadline for those engaged in lobbying is April 14, 2017, so your prompt consideration is appreciated.

Contact Melissa Haas, Acting Director of Federal Relations, at (217)244-4815 if you have any questions or need more information about the LDA.
