The Office of the Vice President for Economic Development and Innovation (VPEDI) announces travel support to encourage the development of new or the strengthening of existing faculty and college collaborations with Israel and Mexico. VPEDI is offering up to 15 travel grants to further the mission of VPEDI, the Discovery Partners Institute (DPI), and the Illinois Innovation Network (IIN). Applications for the grants can be found here: http://go.uillinois.edu/VPEDI_International_Travel_Grants.
