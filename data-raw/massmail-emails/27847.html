Dear Colleagues,

If you are teaching on campus this fall, here are some safety information resources to share with your students. 

  1. A one-page PDF with instructions on what to do in emergencies — designed to be included with your course syllabus or distributed on the first day of class: http://police.illinois.edu/dpsapp/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/syllabus-attachment.pdf
  2. A 60-second script that can be read to your students the first day of class:  http://police.illinois.edu/emergency-preparedness/run-hide-fight/resources-for-instructors/
  3. A two-minute video you can play for your students the first day of class: http://police.illinois.edu/emergency-preparedness/run-hide-fight/

Please use the resources as you find them most useful. If you have any questions or would like more information, please contact Lt. Todd Short of the Division of Public Safety at 244-4877.

Have a safe and productive semester.
