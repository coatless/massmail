Illinois Online – Summer 2015 Courses

The University of Illinois is pleased to announce the Illinois Online Summer 2015 course offerings. More than 300 online course sections in a variety of subject areas are open for summer enrollment. Online courses are taught by Illinois instructors and available in the fall, spring, and summer terms. Please visit the Summer 2015 Checklist for more information about enrolling in Summer 2015 online courses.

Summer 2015 Online Courses offer: 

  • Access to General Education Credits or Coursework to Complete a Degree
  • 4-Week, 8-Week, and 12-Week Formats with a Variety of Start Date Options
  • Opportunity to Learn Time Management skills, Set goals, and Take an Active Role in Degree completion.
  • Flexibility to Accommodate Busy Student Schedules
  • Innovative Approaches to Learning
  • Professional Development  

Explore Summer 2015 Online Course Offerings:

  1. Go to Search Course Explorer
  2. Select Year: 2015
  3. Select Term: Summer
  4. Check: Online
  5. Select: Search

Note: A list of Summer 2015 online course subjects will display on the screen. You may define your search by adding “Gened” or “Part of Term” to your selection. Check back often as new courses are added daily.
