Energy Alert: Reduce Campus Demand

Due to the extreme heat and the increased energy demand, Ameren and Midwest Independent Transmission System Operator, Inc. (MISO) have requested the campus take measures to immediately reduce energy consumption. Conserve energy by turning off/unplugging all non-essential equipment and lights. It is important to continue these extra measures until the high temperatures subside.
