All faculty, staff, graduate and professional students are invited to attend one of three informational sessions on the upcoming transition from the current campus telephone system to Unified Communications. Attendees will learn about the impact of UC Voice on the individual user, how to prepare for the campus-wide change, the timeline for implementation, as well as the resources offered to assist users during the transition.

These identical 90-minute sessions will be held on Monday, November 14 at 9 a.m., 1 p.m., and 3 p.m. in Illini Union Rooms A, B and C. UC Device demonstrations will directly follow each session.

For more information on this event, and all future UC at Illinois information, see the new UC Blog at http://go.illinois.edu/ucblog.
