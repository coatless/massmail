Dear Colleagues,

I am pleased to announce the release of the Visioning Future Excellence at Illinois website  www.oc.illinois.edu/visioning.  The website archives the activities of the Visioning Sessions, features announcements related to the initiative, including the Research University in the World of the Future Lecture Series, and includes a link to the Visioning Survey.  The website will also be a resource for the results of the survey once all of the data have been processed.  

Please visit the website to learn more about what issues have been raised by the groups during the sessions and to take the survey if you have not already done so.  I look forward to posting future updates on this site.

Phyllis M. Wise
Vice President, University of Illinois
Chancellor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
