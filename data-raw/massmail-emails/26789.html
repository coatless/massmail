During the current winter storm, Facilities & Services is making every effort to keep the campus accessible while addressing snow and ice removal. For more information, visit the snow removal guidelines: http://www.fs.uiuc.edu/pdfs/maintenance/snow%20ice%20removal%20guidelines_.pdf For details regarding parking on campus during a winter storm, visit the Parking Department web site at: http://www.parking.illinois.edu

Careful planning and preparation are necessary to maintain a safe environment because of the potential hazard a winter storm event can pose to the students, faculty, staff, and general public who access our campus daily. Your patience and consideration are appreciated while crews do their best to clear snow and ice from our streets, sidewalks, lots and entrances.

If you have questions or concerns about the removal of snow or ice at your building, contact the Service Office at 333-0340.
