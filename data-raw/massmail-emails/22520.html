Dear Faculty Members:

This message is to request nominations for membership on the Faculty Advisory Committee (http://www.fac.illinois.edu/). Any tenured member of the faculty is eligible to serve on the Committee, except those who hold an administrative appointment. The Faculty Advisory Committee (FAC) consists of nine tenured faculty members; no more than two may hold paid appointments in the same college.

This spring, three members are to be elected for three-year terms, August 16, 2012 - August 15, 2015. One additional member will be elected to fill one vacancy on the committee for a term ending August 2013. Below is the current committee membership.
