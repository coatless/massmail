University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign Parking Department to make location change 

The University of Illinois Parking Department will serve motorists from a new location beginning Monday, October 20, 2014. The department is moving from its current location in the Public Safety Building at 1110 W. Springfield Ave., Champaign to a new location at 1201 W. University Ave., Urbana. Situated at the northwest corner of the North Parking Deck, the new office is directly across the street from the Perkins Restaurant and just east of the Beckman Institute. Metered parking is available in the parking deck.

"The Parking Department is an important resource, both for the campus community and the broader community at large," said Michelle Wahl, Director of the Parking Department. "The new location is highly visible and accessible for our customers, and moving to another university-owned property is a cost-effective way to free up additional space."
