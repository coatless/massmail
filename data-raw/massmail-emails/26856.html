Dear Student,
Enrollment in the 2013-2014 Payment Plan is available.  If you would like to utilize the 12 month Annual or the 6 month Fall Semester Payment Plan option, the enrollment deadline is June 27, 2013.  After June 27th, options available will be 11, 10 and 9 month Annual Payment Plans, or 5, 4 and 3 month Fall Semester Payment Plans.

Enrollment in the Payment Plan is optional and is an alternative to the regular University payment policy.  Payment Plan payments are automatically deducted from your checking or savings account electronically each month.  For more information regarding eligibility requirements and enrollment instructions, visit our Payment Plan web page at: http://paymybill.uillinois.edu/PaymentPlan.

Please disregard this message if you are currently enrolled in the Annual 2013-2014 or Fall 2013 Payment Plan.
