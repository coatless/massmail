November 9, 2016

Dear faculty and staff colleagues:

At Thursday’s Board of Trustees meeting, President Tim Killeen will make a presentation on groundbreaking new state legislation that would provide adequate, multi-year funding for the University of Illinois System in exchange for tangible performance goals that support Illinois students and serve the needs of the state.

The meeting begins at 8 a.m. Thursday in Chicago and his presentation on the proposed U of I Investment, Performance, and Accountability Commitment (IPAC) is expected about 11 a.m. You can watch live at http://www.uis.edu/technology/uislive.html

In case you are unable to watch, President Killeen will send a massmail following Thursday’s meeting that will describe the proposal and include a link to a new webpage being developed with more details on IPAC.

Best,

Barbara J. Wilson
Executive Vice President/Vice President for Academic Affairs
