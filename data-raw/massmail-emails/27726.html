Click Here To Vote

Voting is now open for the Spring 2016 Student Elections and Referendum at http://vote.illinois.edu. The ballot is open until 11:59PM on Thursday, March 10.

The following offices are up for election:

    • Student Trustee for the Urbana Campus
    • Student Organization Resource Fee Board
    • Student Senators

Three student-initiaed fees are up for renewal:

    • Student Organization Resource Fee
    • Study Abroad and Travel Scholarship Fee
    • Media Fee

The ballot can be accessed on any computer with internet access; you do not need to be on the University network. During peak times, the ballot may be slow to load. Common technical questions are addressed on the Voting FAQ page. If you have any additional questions or comments, you may e-mail studentelections@illinois.edu.

Thank you for participating in this important part of shared governance at the University of Illinois.

In Orange and Blue,
The Campus Student Election Commission

Students will need to login using their NetID and Active Directory Password. If you need to reset your password, you can do so at IAM Password Manager. Students can only vote once with their NetIDs. If you believe that your password has been lost or stolen or that your University account has been accessed by someone other than you, please contact the Technology Services Help Desk at +1 (217) 244-7000 or consult@illinois.edu to protect your account.
