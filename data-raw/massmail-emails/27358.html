Dear Student,

Enrollment in the Spring 2015 Semester Payment Plan will end on March 2, 2015. Current plans available include the 4 month plan with an enrollment deadline of February 27, 2015, and the 3 month plan with an enrollment deadline of March 2, 2015.

Enrollment in the Payment Plan is optional and is an alternative to the regular University payment policy. Payment Plan payments are automatically deducted from your checking or savings account electronically each month. For more information regarding eligibility requirements and enrollment instructions, visit our Payment Plan web page at: https://paymybill.uillinois.edu/PaymentPlan.

Please disregard this message if you have already paid your student account balance, or are currently enrolled in the Annual 2014-2015 or Spring 2015 Semester Payment Plan.
