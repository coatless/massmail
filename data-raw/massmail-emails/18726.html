Dear Campus Community:

I want to invite you to a campus-wide Town Hall Meeting on Dec. 6 at 4:00 p.m. in the Colwell Playhouse at the Krannert Center for the Performing Arts. The purpose of the meeting is twofold: to provide a preliminary summary of what I have gathered in my ongoing Listening and Learning Tour and, secondly, to hear from you as to what our untapped potential is and how we can best position this campus to achieve even greater successes in the years to come.  This is the first of what I believe will be regular meetings in which we can all participate in our university's future.

This is an approved event, so I hope to see you there!

Phyllis Wise
Vice President, University of Illinois
Chancellor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
