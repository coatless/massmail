Dear Student,

Enrollment in the Spring 2012 Payment Plan is available.  If you would like to utilize the 3 month Payment Plan option, the enrollment deadline is March 8, 2012.  After March 8th, no more Spring plans will be available.

Enrollment in the Payment Plan is optional and is an alternative to the regular University payment policy.  Payment Plan payments are automatically deducted from your checking or savings account electronically each month.  For more information regarding eligibility requirements and enrollment instructions, visit our Payment Plan web page at: http://paymybill.uillinois.edu/PaymentPlan.html.

Please disregard this message if you are currently enrolled in the Annual 2011-2012 Payment Plan.
