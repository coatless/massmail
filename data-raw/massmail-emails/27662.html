Winter Session 2015-2016

Winter Session 2015-2016 begins December 21, 2015 and ends January 15 2016. It’s not too late to enroll in a class!  Deadline to add a winter session class via Student Self-Service is Monday, December 20, 2015 at 11:59 CST.

 

2015-2016 Winter Session Online Courses offer

  • Access to high demand general education courses
  • An opportunity to stay on pace or accelerate towards graduation
  • Rigorous coursework in a condensed 4-week format 
  • An opportunity to focus on a single course
